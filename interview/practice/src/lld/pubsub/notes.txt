Step 1 — Topic creation
service.createTopic("orders")

Creates:
Topic -> orders


Step 2 — Subscribers subscribe
service.subscribe("orders", sub1)
service.subscribe("orders", sub2)

Topic now has:
subscribers = [sub1, sub2]


Step 3 — Publisher publishes message
publisher.publish("orders", message)


Step 4 — PubSubService receives request
publish(topicName, message)


Step 5 — Topic publishes message
topic.publish(message)


Step 6 — Notify subscribers
subscriber.consume(message)

Output:
AnalyticsService received message: Order Created
NotificationService received message: Order Created



When we publish things then a specific topic is published and all of its subscribers get the message.
